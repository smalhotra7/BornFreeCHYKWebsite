Frequently Asked Questions

All the answers you need.

1. Who can sign up for this retreat?

This retreat is open to youth from 12th grade to the age of 28. Exceptions can be made on a case by case basis.

2. What should I bring?

Bring the usual stuff (clothes for three days, toiletries), like you're staying in a hotel. Make sure to bring some comfortable clothing to do yoga and play sports in. If you need sunscreen, bring that as well since we'll be outside for some of our activities. And if you play a musical instrument, feel free to bring that as well!

3. What is the cost of attending?

The full price of the camp, including lodging for two nights and all meals, is $85 early bird, and $90 regular. That's all taken care of when you register.

4. What's up with these two different venues?

Don't fret. We'll take care of you. Our venue for Friday evening will be the Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta Ashram and the venue for Saturday and Sunday will be the Simpsonwood Lodge. Once you arrive at the ashram on Friday to register, we'll take care of the rest. See getting here for more info.

5. What's the lodging situation?

On Friday night, lodging will be provided by members of Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta in their homes. On Saturday night, lodging will be at the Simpsonwood Lodge. All rooms are quad occupancy.

6. What is CHYK and Chinmaya Mission?

Chinmaya Mission is an organization dedicated to providing individuals the wisdom of Vedanta for their spiritual growth and happiness and to enable them to become positive contributors to society. Chinmaya Yuva Kendra (CHYK) is the youth wing of Chinmaya Mission that strives to unite young adults by providing a forum to explore, discuss, and practice Vedanta, while strengthening their lives in every endeavor. CHYK Alpharetta is the CHYK chapter based out of Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta, located in Alpharetta, Georgia.

7. One more thing...do I have to believe in any certain religious philosophy to participate?

Not at all! This is an experience for you to better yourself and the world around you. If that sounds like something that you want to do, then you're set!
