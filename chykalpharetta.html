CHYK Alpharetta

About us.

Who we are

CHYK Alpharetta is the CHYK chapter based out of Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta. CHYK Alpharetta spans Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta Balavihar students in 11th and 12th grade, college students across Georgia universities, and young professionals. CHYK Alpharetta is a continuation of the same principles learned in Balavihar in a self-conducted and practical forum to strengthen the youth and make them dynamic contributors to society. Activities include weekly discussions, social activities, community services, workshops, and retreats like this one.

High School

CHYK programs begin in 9th grade as part of Balavihar. Students in 9th-11th grade are part of Junior CHYK (JCHYK), while 12th graders become JCHYK leaders and CHYKs themselves. Contact Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta for more information.

College

There are CHYK Alpharetta members all over the major Georgia universities including the University of Georgia, Georgia Tech, Georgia State, Emory University, and Mercer University. Currently, there exists an active discussion group at Georgia Tech. If you are a Georgia Tech student and are interested in getting involved, contact us for more information. If you are a student at any of these other universities, contact us to find out about other CHYKs in your area and get a study group started!

And Beyond

The national CHYK program offers activities for young professionals as well from discourses to retreats. Currently, CHYK Alpharetta does not have any discussion groups for young professionals, but if you are interested, please contact us so we can put you in touch with other young professionals in the area and get a group started. There are also a multitude of opportunities offered by Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta such as multiple day discourses, workshops, and retreats.
