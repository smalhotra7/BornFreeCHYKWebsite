Flying In

Book your flights to the Hartsfield Jackson Atlanta International Airport and please indicate that you are flying in when you register. You will be personally contacted by a member of our team and will be asked for flight details so we can pick you up from the airport. After that, you're all set! We'll make sure you're back in time for your flight on Sunday.

Driving In

We're starting our retreat at the Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta ashram, located at 10725 Jones Bridge Road, Johns Creek, GA 30022. Meet us there for registration. That night, lodging will be provided for you with one of our sevaks. However, you do have the option of spending the night at your place and meeting us at Simpsonwood Lodge on Saturday morning.
