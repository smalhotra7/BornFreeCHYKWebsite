Activities & Schedule

Putting thoughts in action.

| Daily Overview

Friday

Arrive and register by 6 PM. Friday evening will consist of meeting the other attendees, dinner, and introducing the theme of the retreat through discussions and activities.

Saturday

Get ready for a full day! Saturday will consist of the bulk of our activities, from sports and games to discourses and discussions. Acharya Vivek will deliver two one-hour discourses throughout the day and we will have several other activities going on related to the theme of the retreat.

Sunday

Acharya Vivek will deliver his third discourse and after a half-day of activities, the retreat will wrap up with closing events by 2 PM.
